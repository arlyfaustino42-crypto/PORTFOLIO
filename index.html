Artist Statement

My artistic work explores the connection between everyday digital experiences and personal identity. I combine elements from social media, design, and visual storytelling to create compositions that reflect modern life. Through both traditional and digital media, I focus on clarity, balance, and meaningful visual communication.

Each piece in this portfolio shows my growth in technique and creativity—from basic observations to more refined and conceptual designs. I value experimentation, attention to detail, and continuous improvement in my process

This portfolio represents not only my skills but also my perspective as a student navigating art in a digital world.

Module Outputs

Module 1: Digital Collage

This digital collage serves as a visual exploration of a daily digital environment, synthesized through the lens of visual literacy and core design principles. The composition utilizes 10 distinct visual elements to represent the intersection of personal identity and the most frequently utilized digital platforms.

Module 2: Observational Drawing

This is a pencil sketch depicting a curated arrangement of modern personal devices and items. The composition is focused on form and subtle tonal shading to capture a clean, minimal aesthetic

Module 3: Abstract Composition

This digital graphic piece explores the relationship between color theory and geometric harmony. The composition is built around a central focal point, utilizing a palette of muted grays, teals, and a singular pop of warm orange to create a balanced, modern aesthetic.

Module 4: Transformation Series

three distinct levels of artistic representation. By using a fish as the central motif, this piece highlights the transition from literal observation to pure geometric form.

Module 5: Filipino Patterns

This creative work explores the intersection of traditional script and modern typographic elements, framed within a geometric border. The piece emphasizes cultural heritage through the use of indigenous lettering while maintaining a balanced, organized layout. Module 5

Module 6-7: Drawing/Painting

This graphite pencil drawing is a classic still life study that explores spatial depth, volume, and the interaction of light on organic forms. The composition focuses on a bowl of fruit set within a domestic environment, demonstrating foundational skills in observational drawing.

Module 8: Digital Poster

is a social advocacy piece designed to communicate a message of environmental sustainability. The composition combines illustrative elements with structured typography to create a clear and impactful visual narrative.

Module 10: IT Infographic

This is a comprehensive educational tool designed to simplify complex cybersecurity concepts for a general audience. The layout combines data visualization, process mapping, and instructional design to deliver a high-impact awareness message.
